Offbeat Goa

1

Fontainhas

Fontainhas is an old Latin quarter situated in Panaji. The Portuguese influence reflects in every bit of its architecture. Here, you get to see some really colorful houses with red-tiled roofs that make you feel like you are taking a stroll in the streets of Europe. This is perhaps one of the major reasons why you will find this place absolutely stunning. The overall look and feel of this place strongly resemble that of the Mediterranean city.

Location: Panaji City, Goa

This place is located in heart Panaji City.

2

Dudhsagar Waterfall

Dudhsagar Falls of Goa is a majestic waterfall where the white water gushing through the steep and vertical face of the mountain portrays a cascading effect. Standing as tall as 310 metres, it makes sure to stand in the list of tallest waterfalls in India. The cascading effect of this waterfall gives rise to the illusion of a milky white sea falling from such a great height.

Location: Sonaulim, Goa

It is 60 km from Panaji by road and is located on the Belgavi–Vasco Da Gama rail route about 46 km east of Madgaon.

3

Coconut Tree Road

The village of Parra is in the Bardez sub-district of North Goa in India. The town of Mapusa is close by, as are the villages of Saligao, Calangute, Assagaon, Anjuna, and Vagator. This lovely place is about 6 kilometers from Baga beach, 5 kilometers from Mapusa City, and 15 kilometers from Panaji City.

Location: Parra, North Goa

This place is easily accessible from popular beaches in North Goa. It is about 6kms away from Calangute Beach.

4

Arambol Beach View Point

If you are wishing to spend some moments in solitude appreciating the exquisiteness of a pristine coastline, then this place is the perfect to be in Goa. It is relatively unexploited by tourists and perfect spot for going to enjoy a view with your folks.

Location: Arambol, North Goa

It is northern part of North Goa District, about 25kms from Calangute Beach.

5

Cabo de Rama Fort & Beach

The magnificent fort overlooks the gorgeous Arabian Sea; thus, offers a striking contrast to the ruins of this ancient site. In fact, this fort is popular for presenting the visitors with the best mesmerizing sunset views. No doubt, on touring the ancient monument, you'll return as a vibrant person enriched with the history the fort depicts and gifted by the beauty all around it.

Location: Canacona, South Goa

It is located in South Goa & 60km from Panjim.

6

Butterfly Beach

The place occupies a tiny semicircular bay on the high northern end of Palolem beach and the most astounding feature is that you can spot millions of butterflies flying over the hilltop blossoms. The golden sands against the warm blue water create postcard perfect location with palm trees and scores of butterflies.

Location: Palolem, South Goa

To visit this place either you can take ferry from Palolem Beach or you can choose to go by road to butterfly beach but you have to take trail for last 2kms.
